==========================================
Observation: order_delivered_customer_date
==========================================

Missing Values Found:
order_delivered_customer_date -> 2965 missing values

Investigation:
Analyzed order_status for rows where
order_delivered_customer_date is missing.

Findings:
shipped      -> 1107
canceled     -> 619
unavailable  -> 609
invoiced     -> 314
processing   -> 301
delivered    -> 8
created      -> 5
approved     -> 2

Conclusion:
Most missing delivery dates correspond to orders that
were not completed or not yet delivered, which is a
valid business scenario.

Special Case:
8 orders have status='delivered' but still have a
missing delivery date. This appears to be a data
quality issue.

Decision:
No imputation performed.
Missing values will be retained because the actual
delivery date is unknown and should not be replaced
with estimated delivery dates.

For delivery-time analysis, rows with missing
actual delivery dates may be excluded.
